Legend has it...

Born into a family of assassins in the shadow of Bleachbone Keep and set upon a dark path of revenge by the treachery of neighboring warlords, the pale lad known as Nigel Untzalot eventually found his bitter victory atop a windswept hill in the wild Under regions. Standing tall among the stacked corpses of his enemies, Nigel gazed upon the blood matting their long fuzzy ears, pink noses, and puffball tails. He felt no joy then, only the singular thud of his youth's gates as they closed within him. His father's blade felt heavy in his grip, and without emotion he let it fall to the crushed grass. Never again would he hear its song as it whistled through the air. There were more powerful weapons to be mastered. The oracle had spoken of a form of war magic, an immense sorcery that made the very air wail, ground pulse, breast tighten, and soul shiver. Nigel's thoughts hearkened back to the withered sage's cackle, wheezing through dusty lips as one last warning was bestowed. "Hand, head, and heart, young Untzalot! Each plays its part in the Dragon's Art, and mind you never let one wither! Hand, head, and heart!" Nigel glanced up into the night's eternity and patted the precious black-wrapped crate slung at his side, which his travels had already filled with a modest supply of mystical ingredients. If the old fraud spoke truly, and Nigel indeed meant to learn the ways of the Draco Jinni, then he was going to need a bigger crate.
